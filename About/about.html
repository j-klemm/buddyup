What is BuddyUp?

BuddyUp is a tool to help groups of friends plan and save for regular trips by solving the biggest hurdles to planning a group trip: deciding on a budget and getting everybody to commit. By adding money to the group’s funding, members commit to the trip early, so the planning process gets easier after that. Safe, secure, and fun payments let users see how much they’ve raised and how much is left to go to help easily fund their trips by making payments - big or small. Our mission is to help groups of people stay friends for life by allowing them to finally follow through on the plans they want to make with each other.


How does it work?

Users create a trip with a destination and add their friends to the trip group. Next comes the most important part! Each group member starts contributing to the cost of the trip until the goal amount is met. The funds are stored until the trip is fully funded, or the user requests to cash out the funds contributed.


Technical Summary

BuddyUp is a platform for groups of friends to coordinate funding for their trips. Users create accounts, and anyone can create a trip to invite their friends. When invited to a trip, users can decline or accept the invitation. Payment is handled with the Stripe API. Trip data and user data are stored in the COMP426 backend, and cash outs can be made to the trip host at any point to retrieve the pooled money.


Backend Implementation

The public datastore holds a list of users with the trips they are part of. Payment details and contributions to trips are held in the `user` datastore. Trip data is held in the private datastore. The trip data is a list of trip objects containing the host name, list of members who are in the trip and have accepted the invite, a list of members who have been invited but not yet accepted the trip invite, the amount requested for the trip, and the amount raised. Each user can see how much they have contributed but cannot view how much others have contributed.


Stripe API

Stripe builds the most powerful and flexible tools for internet commerce. Whether you’re creating a subscription service, an on-demand marketplace, an e-commerce store, or a crowdfunding platform, Stripe’s meticulously designed APIs and unmatched functionality help you create the best possible product for your users. Payment is handled entirely by stripe for security purposes. Users can choose how much they want to contribute on the BuddyUp site and are redirected to Stripe to pay that amount using a RESTful endpoint. Money is held in a stripe account and can be deposited into the host’s stripe or bank account upon cash out.
